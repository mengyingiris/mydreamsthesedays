About the Event

My Dreams These Days is a 12-hour showcase of student artist work streamed consecutively online as well as projected live at physical locations in the Chicago area.
This student run, marathon event is a combined virtual and physical experience, with an emphasis on time based art pieces, including but not limited to sound, film, new media, art and technology, performance, time-based sculpture, etc. The event will consist of three curated stages with a focus on the followingconceptual prompts: Material, Virtual and Rhythmic. The goal of this student artist collective is meant to facilitate a space for sharing creative visions in a post-pandemic context while opening the possibility for an ongoing online archive for community building and discussion.
